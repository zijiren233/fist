The chart deploys the in-cluster components only.
To fully enable OIDC + authorization webhook, kube-apiserver must still be configured manually with:

  --authorization-mode=Node,Webhook,RBAC
  --authorization-webhook-config-file=/etc/kubernetes/pki/fist/authz-webhook.kubeconfig
  --oidc-issuer-url=https://fist.sealyun.svc.cluster.local:8443
  --oidc-client-id=sealyun-fist
  --oidc-ca-file=/etc/kubernetes/pki/fist/ca.pem
  --oidc-username-claim=name
  --oidc-groups-claim=groups
  --oidc-username-prefix=-
  --oidc-groups-prefix=-

Extract the CA certificate for kube-apiserver with:

  kubectl get secret {{ include "fist.auth.tlsSecretName" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -o jsonpath='{.data.ca\.pem}' | base64 -d
